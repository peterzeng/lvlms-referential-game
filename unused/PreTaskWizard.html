Task Instructions

  1. You are on a team with a partner. Your goal is to work with your teammate to match the correct order of a set of baskets. The game consists of 4 rounds, and in each round, your team must correctly order 12 baskets.
  2. There are two distinct roles in this task: the Director and the Matcher. Both partners see the same 12 baskets; The Matcher sees a few extras.
    • Director: Will see the correct target sequence for the 12 baskets and needs to type clear descriptions of each basket one by one (in order starting with the upper-left basket) to the Matcher via a live chat.
    • Matcher: Will see these 12 target baskets plus some other baskets. As the Director describes each basket, the Matcher should interpret the Director's description, ask clarifying questions if needed, and select the correct target basket by clicking on it. The basket will move up.
  3. The two of you can communicate back and forth as much as needed to do the task. If you discover an error, it is fine to make corrections within a round. When the round is finished, the Matcher should submit the sequence of 12 baskets, and you'll both see your score. The study consists of 4 rounds in total.
  4. Please have your sound on; you'll hear a chime when your partner types something.
  5. Make sure your screen is maximized so you can see all the task details.
  6. Please don't navigate off this site until you've completed the task.
